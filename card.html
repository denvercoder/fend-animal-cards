German Shepherd Dog

German Shepherds have one of the strongest bites of all dog species.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 66 - 88lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 9 - 13 years
  • Habitat: Houses and Junkyards

I like German Shepherds because they are protective and yet they don't look as menacing as some of the other protection dogs like dobermans and rottweilers.
